Homeowner Programs

Comfort and care, built in.

Two programs are included in every Southbury home — because the way a home performs, and the way it looks after you, should never be an afterthought.

Harmony — Environmental Performance

Harmony Package

A happier, healthier home.

Southbury Homes believes in providing a happier, healthier home for you and your family, built in a way that will also benefit future generations. Our Harmony Program ensures our homes are built to a higher environmental standard — helping you save money, reduce your carbon footprint, and have a home you can comfortably raise your family in.

  • Heat Recovery Ventilator (HRV) for fresher air throughout your home
  • Low-VOC paints for better indoor air quality
  • Low-flow toilets, faucets and shower heads
  • LED bulbs
  • 96% high-efficiency natural gas furnace
  • ENERGY STAR® qualified smart thermostat
  • …and more

The Southbury Homes Harmony Package is our commitment to smarter, more sustainable living. Featuring energy-efficient technologies, water-saving innovations, and responsibly sourced materials, it is designed to enhance everyday comfort, reduce environmental impact, and create healthier homes for generations to come.

Comfort+ — Intelligent Living

Comfort+ Package

The future, at your fingertips.

The Southbury Homes Comfort+ Package brings the future of homeownership to your fingertips. Thoughtfully designed to enhance comfort, convenience, security, and efficiency, it empowers homeowners with intelligent technology that simplifies everyday living. Because luxury isn't just about where you live — it's about how you live. With Comfort+, your home becomes smarter, more responsive, and effortlessly connected to the way you live today.

  • A smart central Automation Hub with touchscreen at the front entrance of the home
  • A smart door lock on the inside garage door leading to the house (grade/options permitting)
  • Smart lighting control
  • Smart thermostat control
  • Smart water-leak sensor
