Décor Studio

Your home, your story.

At Southbury Homes, our Interior Design & Décor Team delivers a highly personalized, boutique-style experience that transforms every house into a distinctive and timeless home.

We believe great design extends far beyond aesthetics — it begins with understanding how you live.

Guided by a passion for exceptional design and meticulous attention to detail, our designers work closely with each homeowner to create interiors that are both beautiful and deeply reflective of their lifestyle.

It begins with understanding our client's vision, aspirations, and the way they live. Through thoughtful collaboration, curated selections, and expert guidance, we bring together architecture, finishes, textures, and furnishings to create spaces that feel effortlessly elegant and uniquely personal.
